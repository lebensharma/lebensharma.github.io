Introduction

Pinga (“Application”) is a Safety / Communication app developed by Aashutosh Sharma (“Service Provider”). The Application is provided free of charge and intended for use AS IS.

Your privacy and safety are top priorities. Pinga has no backend server, uses no analytics, shows no ads, and does not share your personal information with third parties. All data stays on your device and you remain in control at all times.

Information Collection and Use

  • Pinga does not collect or transmit personal data to any external server.
  • The Application functions entirely on your device. SMS messages are sent from your SIM card to contacts you select.
  • You can add or remove contacts, set per-contact restrictions, and start/stop the app at any time.
  • Access to the app is protected by your device’s screen lock/biometrics (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock).
  • The Service Provider has no visibility into which contacts you choose or the content of messages sent by your device.

Note If you wish to report a bug, you may submit a review with a screenshot on the app store or email the developer. This is entirely voluntary and initiated by you.

Permissions and Their Usage

The Application requests certain permissions solely to enable core safety features. You can revoke any permission in your device settings; some features may then be unavailable.

Location (Foreground & Background)

Used to include your location in outgoing SMS so loved ones can know your approximate whereabouts. If disabled, location-based features will not work.

SMS

Used to automatically send an SMS after a missed call. Messages are sent directly from your device’s SIM; Pinga does not transmit or store copies on any server.

Phone State

Used to detect missed calls and trigger the optional SMS workflow.

Call Log

Used to identify the missed call to send the appropriate notification to your chosen contact(s). If a contact is blocked, Pinga will not send messages to that contact.

Contacts

Used so you can select recipients for messages. Pinga does not upload or share your contact list anywhere.

Notifications

Used to inform you about sent SMS and active features, and to surface important status information.

Battery Optimization

Used to request exclusion from battery optimizations so background features remain reliable.

Data Retention

  • All data remains stored locally on your device.
  • Uninstalling the Application permanently deletes app data and settings from your device.
  • The Service Provider does not retain or access user data.

Third-Party Access

  • The Application does not share data with third-party companies or services.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are integrated.
  • Feedback you submit (e.g., via app store review or email) is voluntary and sent by you.

Security

The Application relies on your device’s built-in protections (screen lock and biometric authentication) for access control. Since no external servers or cloud storage are used, your data never leaves your device.

Children’s Privacy

Pinga is a utility app with no specific target age group and is often used as a safety aid (e.g., by mothers for their children). The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Your Consent

By using Pinga, you consent to the handling of information as described in this Privacy Policy. Because there is no backend, “processing” occurs solely on your device.
